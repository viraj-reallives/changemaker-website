RealLives Foundation is proud to certify
The Orchid School Pune as a ChangeMaker School

After the successful completion of the Changemaker Program at The Orchid School, Pune, RealLives is proud to certify the school as an official Changemaker School. This year, the program engaged 75 students, resulting in 10 unique social business pitches and movements, along with 75 individual Changemaker Reports showcasing thoughtful, impact-driven ideas.

This achievement reflects the school's commitment to nurturing leadership, empathy, and social responsibility in young minds.
